Junk Food Research Insights

Exploring the consumption habits and health impacts of junk food over time.


Our Agenda




Collected data from various fast food chains, offering a wide range of food items. We also considered sample food items options from food outlets. We observed eating habits and noted down frequent items ordered, focusing on meals popular among the general public.

Conducted a survey at a local school to understand food preferences. We found that over 60% of the people had junk food more than twice a week.

Analyzed the data of the survey forms from the people. Most snacks exceeded the healthier options and preferred towards refined flour and low-quality oils cooked items.
